Understanding ROI

ROI (Return on Investment) is a performance measure used to evaluate the efficiency or profitability of an investment.

The formula used: ROI = [(Final Value - Total Investment) / Total Investment] Ã— 100

Annualized ROI = [(1 + ROI)^(1/years) - 1] Ã— 100

  • Positive ROI: Your investment has gained value
  • Negative ROI: Your investment has lost value
  • 0% ROI: Break-even, no gain or loss

Use Cases: Stock investments, real estate, business ventures, marketing campaigns, equipment purchases, and any scenario where you want to measure investment efficiency.
